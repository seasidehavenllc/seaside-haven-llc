About Us

Who We Are

Seaside Haven LLC is a family‑owned, multi‑service company serving the Lynchburg and Roanoke region. What began as small home projects has grown into a full lineup of services designed to support families, homeowners, and local businesses with reliable work and long‑lasting results.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: provide dependable services that improve homes, strengthen communities, and make everyday life easier. Whether it’s a remodel, a yard cleanup, a rental property, or a new business venture, we focus on quality, communication, and respect.

Our Services & Growth

Seaside Haven LLC continues expanding into new service lines to meet real needs in our community. From remodeling and landscaping to rental management, vending machines, live commerce, laundromat development, and future Airbnb setups — we’re building a brand that grows with you.

Why Homeowners & Businesses Choose Us

  • Honest Pricing: Clear, upfront quotes with no surprises.
  • Reliable Scheduling: We show up, follow through, and finish strong.
  • Quality Work: Craftsmanship that lasts — no shortcuts.
  • Local Commitment: Family‑owned and community‑driven.
  • Multi‑Service Support: One company for home, property, and business needs.

Seaside Haven LLC is actively expanding into new service lines to meet community needs. Our roadmap includes:
