Welcome to Seaside Haven LLC

Building Homes, Businesses, and Community

About Us

Who We Are

Seaside Haven LLC started as a small family project and grew into a multi‑service business built on trust, hard work, and real results. We believe in doing things the right way — with respect, transparency, and craftsmanship that lasts.

Our Mission

To provide dependable services that improve homes, support families, and strengthen our community. Whether it’s a remodeling project, a rental property, a yard cleanup, or a new business venture, our goal is to deliver value you can see and service you can feel.

Our Growth

What began with home improvement has expanded into landscaping, property management, vending machines, live commerce, laundromat development, and future Airbnb offerings. We’re committed to evolving with the needs of our customers and our community.

Family Owned. Community Driven.

We’re proud to serve the Lynchburg and Roanoke area with a hands‑on approach and a commitment to treating every project like it’s our own. When you work with Seaside Haven LLC, you’re supporting a local business that puts people first.
