About Our Landscaping Services

Seaside Haven LLC provides reliable, clean, and affordable landscaping services for homes and rental properties across the Lynchburg and Roanoke area. From routine lawn care to full outdoor transformations, we keep your yard looking sharp year‑round.

What We Offer
  • Routine Lawn Care: Mowing, trimming, edging, and debris removal.
  • Mulch & Bed Refresh: New mulch, bed cleanup, weed removal, plant care.
  • Seasonal Cleanups: Spring prep, fall leaf removal, storm cleanup.
  • Planting & Shrubs: New shrubs, flowers, small trees, and garden installs.
  • Yard Upgrades: Borders, stonework, small hardscapes, curb‑appeal improvements.
  • Rental Property Care: Reliable maintenance for landlords and tenants.
